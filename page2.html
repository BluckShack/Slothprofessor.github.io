On the 4th of August 1577, legend says a terrible omen visited the small town of Bungay. The townsfolk cowered in St Mary's Church as the rain battered the roof and the thunderous sky exploded above them.

As the sermons went on, more and more, they stopped listening to the toxic words of the preacher, too petrified with fear for they could not escape the dreadful clutches of Catholicism.
